Policy Translation Impact

When translating AWS IAM policies to OCI policies:

  • AWS service names are automatically mapped to their OCI equivalents
  • AWS actions are mapped to OCI verbs and resources
  • AWS condition keys are translated to OCI policy conditions where applicable

For example, s3:GetObject becomes Allow group [group] to read objects in tenancy
